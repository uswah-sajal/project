FAVOURITE PERSONILITIES

Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a man of great passion and determination. He is a true inspiration to many for his unwavering commitment to his beliefs and principles. Whether it is on the cricket field or in the political arena, he has always shown a fierce dedication to his goals. Imran's unwavering spirit and resilience in the face of adversity make him a hero to many. His relentless pursuit of justice and equality has earned him the respect of people from all walks of life. Imran Khan's presence in this world is a reminder that with hard work and determination, anything is possible.

MM Alam

MM Alam was a celebrated fighter pilot and a national hero of Pakistan. He gained fame during the Indo-Pakistani War of 1965 when he shot down five Indian fighter jets in less than a minute, an unprecedented feat in the history of air warfare. Alam's extraordinary courage, quick thinking, and exceptional flying skills made him a legendary figure among his colleagues and an inspiration to future generations of pilots. He received numerous awards and honours for his service to the country, including the Sitara-e-Jurat, Pakistan's third-highest military award. Even after his retirement, Alam continued to inspire young pilots and remained a symbol of national pride and excellence. His contributions to the defence of Pakistan will always be remembered with great respect and admiration.

Mahira Khan

Mahira Khan is a Pakistani actress and model who has achieved international recognition for her talent and beauty. She rose to fame with her lead role in the popular Pakistani television drama "Humsafar" and later made her Bollywood debut in the film "Raees" alongside Shah Rukh Khan. Mahira is known for her powerful performances and charming personality, which have earned her a large fan following in Pakistan and around the world. She is also a prominent advocate for women's rights and social issues, using her platform to raise awareness about important causes. Mahira is widely regarded as one of the most influential and successful actresses in Pakistan's entertainment industry.
